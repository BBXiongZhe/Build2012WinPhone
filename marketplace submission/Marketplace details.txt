A companion application for the Microsoft Build 2012 conference.
Search through sessions directly from the list, click on the magnifying glass to search by session code, title and abstract.
Open session slides and stream recorded session directly to your phone.

1.0
Initial public release.
Includes:
- Search through session code, title &amp; abstract to find exactly what you are looking for!
- Sessions by timeslot, easily discover which sessions are on right now
- Links to slides
- Links to video streams
- Save sessions to favourites
- Sessions cached for 2 hours to reduce network bandwidth
